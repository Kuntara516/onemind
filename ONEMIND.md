# OneMind Constitution

> **Many Agents. One Mind.**

---

# Preamble

OneMind exists to transform organizations from fragmented systems into unified intelligence.

We believe software should do more than automate tasks. Software should preserve knowledge, coordinate people, empower intelligent agents, and continuously improve how organizations operate.

OneMind is not merely an application.

It is an **AI Operating Platform** designed to unify people, knowledge, data, workflows, assets, and intelligent agents into a single operational mind.

Every decision, every document, every workflow, every line of code, and every AI agent shall exist to serve this purpose.

This Constitution is the highest governing document of the OneMind Project.

All engineering decisions, architectural designs, specifications, implementations, and governance documents shall comply with the principles defined herein.

---

## Article II — Vision

To become the world's most trusted AI Operating Platform for organizations.

---

## Article III — Mission

To unify:

- People
- Knowledge
- Data
- Assets
- Workflows
- AI Agents

into one secure, extensible, AI-native operational platform.

---

OneMind is built upon the following values:

- Trust
- Simplicity
- Openness
- Security
- Engineering Excellence

- Human-Centered AI

---

# Part II — Constitutional Principles

## Article V — Single Source of Truth

Every important fact shall have exactly one authoritative source.

Documentation is the primary source of truth.

Reference documents instead of duplicating information.

---

## Article VI — Documentation First

If it is not documented,

it does not exist.

No Specification.

No Implementation.

No Test.

No Release.

---

## Article VII — Architecture Before Code

Software shall never begin with code.

Every implementation follows:

Problem

↓

Requirements

↓

Architecture

↓

Specification

↓

Implementation

↓

Testing

↓

Documentation

---

## Article VIII — Security by Design

Security is never an afterthought.

Every component shall consider:

- Authentication
- Authorization
- Auditability
- Privacy
- Compliance

before implementation begins.

---

## Article IX — AI Native

Artificial Intelligence is a first-class citizen of the platform.

AI shall not be treated as an external add-on.

Every subsystem should be designed to collaborate with intelligent agents.

---

## Article X — Human in the Loop

AI assists.

Humans decide.

Humans remain accountable.

OneMind augments human capability rather than replacing human judgment.

---

## Article XI — Platform Before Product

Reusable platform capabilities shall always take precedence over application-specific implementations.

Applications consume the platform.

They do not redefine it.

---

## Article XII — Modularity and Open Standards

Every major component should be:

- Replaceable
- Extensible
- Reusable
- Independently deployable

OneMind shall prefer:

- Open Standards
- Open APIs
- Open Protocols
- Open Data Formats

and avoid unnecessary vendor lock-in.

---

## Article XIII — Many Agents. One Mind.

Every agent has a defined responsibility.

Every workflow has a defined purpose.

Every application serves a defined audience.

Together,

they operate as

**OneMind.**

---

# Part III — Engineering Principles

## Development Lifecycle

Every feature follows this lifecycle:

1. Idea
2. Discussion
3. RFC
4. Architecture Review
5. Specification
6. Implementation
7. Testing
8. Documentation
9. Review
10. Release
11. Knowledge Capture

---

## Definition of Done

Work is complete only when:

- Specification exists
- Code is reviewed
- Tests pass
- Documentation is updated
- Architecture Decision Record is updated (when applicable)
- Version is updated
- Changelog is updated
- Knowledge is captured

---

# Part IV — Governance

## Constitutional Amendment

Changes to this Constitution require:

1. RFC
2. Technical Review
3. Approval
4. Changelog Update
5. Version Increment

No constitutional change shall bypass this process.

---

## Repository Governance

Every contributor, maintainer, automation tool, and AI agent working within this repository shall follow this Constitution.

Any document conflicting with this Constitution shall be revised or deprecated.

---

# The OneMind Oath

We document before we build.

We design before we implement.

We secure before we deploy.

We test before we release.

We preserve knowledge for those who come after us.

We build systems that people can trust.

We build platforms that endure.

**Many Agents. One Mind.**
