# Architecture Principles

> **Architecture is the embodiment of business strategy through technology.**

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the architectural constitution of the OneMind AI Operating System.

Architecture Principles are the highest-level governance artifacts within OneMind.

All architecture decisions, engineering standards, implementation guidelines, and technology selections shall conform to these principles.

Any deviation requires formal approval through the Architecture Decision Record (ADR) process.

---

# 2. Architecture Philosophy

The architecture of OneMind is founded on a single overarching principle:

> **A sustainable architecture is achieved through disciplined evolution while preserving conceptual integrity.**

This philosophy governs every architectural decision and serves as the foundation for all subsequent principles.

---

# 3. Foundational Principle

# P-00 Architecture Integrity

## Statement

The architecture shall evolve through disciplined governance while preserving conceptual integrity across the entire platform.

## Rationale

Architecture is a long-term strategic asset.

As OneMind grows across industries, teams, technologies, and deployment environments, every change must strengthen—not fragment—the overall architecture.

A consistent architectural vision enables scalability, maintainability, interoperability, and long-term sustainability.

## Design Implications

- Every architectural change must align with the long-term platform vision.
- Local optimizations must never compromise global architectural consistency.
- New technologies shall adapt to the architecture, not redefine it.
- Architectural evolution is intentional, incremental, and governed.

## Anti-patterns

✗ Technology-driven redesign

✗ Multiple competing architectural styles

✗ Team-specific architecture

✗ Inconsistent terminology

✗ Repository restructuring without governance

✗ Breaking established architectural conventions

## Success Criteria

Architecture Integrity is achieved when:

- All components follow a consistent architectural language.
- Similar problems are solved using consistent patterns.
- Documentation remains coherent across all domains.
- New contributors can understand the platform without conflicting interpretations.
- Architecture evolves without losing its original vision.
